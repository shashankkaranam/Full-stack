FAQs

Find answers to common questions about how our food donation platform operates.

How do I donate?

Just select the 'Feed a Meal' option on our platform, choose the amount you wish to contribute, and complete the payment process. Your donation will directly support local food initiatives.

Who can receive meals?

Anyone in need can receive meals through our platform. We partner with local organizations to ensure that food reaches those who are food insecure.

How can I volunteer?

Sign up on our platform and select the 'Volunteer to Deliver Food' option. You will receive notifications about available delivery opportunities in your area.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve multiple regions across the country. Our network is continually expanding to reach more communities in need.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, your donations are tax-deductible! After your contribution, you will receive a receipt via email for your records.
