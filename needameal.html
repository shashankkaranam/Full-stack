FAQs

Find answers to common questions NGOs have when seeking meals for those in need.

  • How do I apply?

    To apply for meals, NGOs must submit their details through our online form.

  • What information is needed?

    We require basic information such as the NGO's name, contact details, and the number of meals needed.

  • How long does it take?

    Processing your application typically takes 3-5 business days.

  • Can I change my request?

    You can modify your request if your needs change.

  • Who can apply?

    Any registered NGO that supports individuals in need can apply for meals.
